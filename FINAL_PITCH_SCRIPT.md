## Technical & Storytelling Highlights (For Judges)
- **The "Hook"**: Shifts from a formal problem statement to a "Financial Emergency" for a relatable persona (Rohan).
- **The "Innovation"**: Presents the Adversarial Defense as a **major technical feat**, not just a security checklist.
- **The "Why"**: Emphasizes that traditional insurance fails because it's too slow for the gig economy.
- **The "How"**: Mentions specific high-level models (Isolation Forests, Graph Analysis, XGBoost) to show technical depth.

---

## Recording Instructions for a "Top 5%" Pitch
1. **Energy Level**: Start strong! Your voice should convey urgency in the hook and confidence in the fraud section.
2. **Speed**: Keep it brisk but clear. If you find yourself over 120 seconds, cut the "Technology names" in the Roadmap section, but **never** cut the Fraud Innovation part.
3. **The "Rohan" Moment**: Pause for 1 second after mentioning Rohan’s zero income to let the impact sink in.
4. **No Demo**: Focus your hand gestures and eye contact on the camera (or the slides) to show you are a "Product Thinker" and a "System Architect."
